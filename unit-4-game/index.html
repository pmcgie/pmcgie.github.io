Crystal Collector: Indiana Jones Edition

Welcome to the crystal collector game: Indiana Jones Style!

As Indy goes on this Journey to find the crystals in the "Coding Temple of Doom", don't get too greedy! In order to survive and get as many crystals as he wants, he must be calculated on how many he takes!

Rules to the Game:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target score. You lose the game if your score goes above the target number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal as as well as generate a new target value.
